## Translation In English
Title: LA POEM - First Encounter

The crimson sky
With drifting clouds at the far end
The long summer has passed by
Rain embracing memories' scent
When it sprinkles on my eyes
I will suddenly recall
The past seasons
As the sky heightens
And the blue leaves are painted
Like the blowing wind
Will they gently fade away?
On the blushing leaves
Trace of past warmth
In the flowing time
Memories of tears and laughter
In everything, you remain
Though still lingering
Season departing,
On your journey
Leave behind
A flower yet to bloom
With the final rain
Now softly send off
My past brilliance that I loved!
