## Translation In English
Title: Lee Young-hyun & Park Min-hye (Big Mama) - Wish I Could Say I Love You

Content:
Leaving without a word,
I cried for a long time through this night.

Without saying you loved me,
Only painful memories remain
You left me so bitterly.

And as each season changes like that,
I feel the blank space of memories fading away.

Without you, any season
has no meaning at all,
The more I know, the more it hurts because of you.

I wish I could say I love you,
I wish I could tell you before you leave,
No matter how much I love you,
I call out, but it scatters away like that.

In our shared memories,
I will erase and throw away the pain,
Now, I will say I love you.

Even though I laughed as if nothing happened,
Your affectionate words suddenly came to mind.

The days we endured together
Suddenly collapsed in an instant,
And once again, I cried.

I wish I could say I love you,
I wish I could tell you before you leave,
No matter how much I love you,
I call out, but it scatters away like that.

In our shared memories,
I will erase and throw away the pain,
Now, I will say I love you.

In the passed seasons,
With the words of love you said,
Today, once again,
I barely managed to survive the day.

Even if I try harder to erase you,

The words I said, "I love you,"
The words you said before you left,
I still can't forget them like that.

In the memories we shared,
Even the awkward words I occasionally spit out,
Now, I will say I love you.
