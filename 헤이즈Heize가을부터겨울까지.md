## Translation In English
Title: Heize (헤이즈) - From Autumn to Winter

From exactly autumn to winter,
We were together.
Among the seasons, the shortest autumn
And the coldest winter.

The words I wanted to say to you
Couldn't reach you,
Frozen on my cold lips,
On this night lengthened by shadows.
Time will pass again,
From autumn to exactly winter.

From autumn to exactly winter,
You and I wrote the words 'us.'
It's not strange
That I sometimes think of you
Because it's from autumn to exactly winter.

We dreamed of a warm future,
But only the harsh wind waits for us,
To protect our hearts
With only the outerwear we have.

Because I'm afraid,
I walk through the darkness once again.
Even if it's not me anytime,
Be happy for sure.

From autumn to exactly winter,
You and I wrote the words 'us.'
It's not strange
That I sometimes think of you
Because it's from autumn to exactly winter.

Even if scattered, we'll meet,
The snow that turns the world white
Hopes to cover and erase
Even my memories,
Sending you in my breath,
Secretly in the air.

From autumn to exactly winter,
You and I wrote the words 'us.'
It's not strange
That I sometimes think of you
Because it's from autumn to exactly winter.
