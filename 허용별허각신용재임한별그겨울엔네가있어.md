## Translation In English

In the cold wind that pushes me
I move my steps
Facing the memories that were buried
On a night like this

You, who fixed my crooked scarf
Come to my mind like a fool again

On my freezing nose
In my fluttering heart
Your face briefly crosses my mind

Thank you for remembering me
For becoming my winter
At least once
Come down like the first snow

Following the beloved you
When I rewind back to those days
You are there
Still smiling

In the streets filled with lights
When the bells ring
The song of December that we sang together

Thank you for remembering me
For becoming my winter
At least once
Come down like the first snow

Following the beloved you
When I rewind back to those days
You are there
Still smiling

In that memory
In the cold season
I go out to meet you

Quietly accumulating
Even if the snowflakes melt
You will fall again
Into my heart

Following the long night
When I wander to the end of memories
I miss you
We are still there
You are there in that winter
