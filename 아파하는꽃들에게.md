## Translation In English
Title:
- To the Suffering Flowers

Content:
The weakened dreams of the past night,
swaying in the fierce wind,
fading with a faint light,
vanish like that.
Even when I close my eyes to sleep,
again, to the suffering flowers,
the wind will pass by, holding both hands, embracing.
So I promise.
Approaching quietly, embracing gently,
I try to hold your pain in my arms.
At the end of your crying in pain,
even if you seem exhausted,
I softly call your name,
tender and fragile.
On nights when you can't even cry out,
even if it crumbles again,
approaching quietly, embracing gently,
I try to hold your pain in my arms.
May the hope in my heart
meet you tomorrow.
