title:
- Bitter Smile (Different Perspectives)

content:

We had a party like a rockstar, always in chaos

I approach you, saying I'll sing you a song

If I embrace you with a pretty expression
It was supposed to all be okay
I let go with a slightly smiling face
I gave it all, even the heart you gave
Without you, I might die, how stupid I am
One day will just pass by
But in the end, I pushed you away until the very last moment
Now you seem unnecessary
You said you'll comfort my heart all through the night
You consoled me with nice words
Even when I pushed you away with an angry expression
I thought it would be fine
But you remain silent with a very sad expression
I lost everything. I lost even the heart you gave.
