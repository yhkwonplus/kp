## Translation In English
Title:  
Hwang Chi-yeul - A Winter Night

Content:  
With only a dim light on in the empty room,  
I sit and gaze at the first snow falling so white.  

The laughter from the streets  
warms my heart.  

On this night falling like white snow,  
In this winter where we'll be together,  
It's a night where excitement only grows.  

Echoing throughout the streets,  
calling out to you with this song,  
It's a night where my love only grows.  

Wondering if I'll fall asleep, I close my eyes and count,  
I even opened a book I hadn't looked at before.  

Thinking back to the past, I can't help but smile,  
Every moment feels like a dream.  

On this night falling like white snow,  
In this winter where we'll be together,  
It's a night where excitement only grows.  

Echoing throughout the streets,  
calling out to you with this song,  
But my love only grows.  

Another day passes by again today,  
Even as a new morning comes,  
If you're by my side,  
I can do anything,  
I love you.  

At four-thirty in the morning, still unable to sleep,  
Just tossing and turning in bed,  
It's a night where excitement only grows.  

Echoing throughout the streets,  
calling out to you with this song,  
It's a night where my love only grows.
