I saw you looking happy.
I watched you quietly.

Inadequate, unable,
Words I couldn't say to you,
I still love you.

As the love that sets with the sunset calls me,
Standing in place,
I shout to you,

Love that merely grazed like the wind,
I hope for your happiness, for that love.
Even if it cuts deep and hurts,
I pray for your happiness.

Me, still loving you,
It's not a prayer, but a prayer.

Your smile, even without me,
I only gaze, like a fool.

A world without you calls me,
The end, the conclusion,
I stand at the edge of a cliff.

Love that merely grazed like the wind,
I hope for your happiness, for that love.
Even if it cuts deep and hurts,
I pray for your happiness.

It's not a prayer, but a prayer.
Your smile, even without me,
I only gaze.

Secretly reversing time,
Rewinding, wrapping up,
I will love you again.

Time flows and passes,

Trapped in the shadow,
Even if I pass you by.

Me, still loving you,
It's not a prayer, but a prayer.
