## Translation In English
Title: Forestella - Words Carried by the Wind
Content:
One day, the passing wind
Carries a message to me
Lifting my head buried in deep sorrow
It tells me to look up at the sky
Like the birds soaring high above
It says not to dwell on yesterday
Leaving behind the dazzling and heartbreaking memories here
Live for today, it says
I wonder where I am now
And where I should be heading
Having passed through those countless days
Every day still feels distant as if it were the first
Like the birds soaring high above
It tells me not to worry about tomorrow
Just as flowers bloom and wither in their place
Blooming once again
If you're alive, live with all your might, it says
Every time I fall with wounds
The wind pushes me forward and delivers the message
Now, I feel like I might understand that message a little
The long sorrows
Were gifts from the world
To help me realize happiness
Now, at the end of the long wandering
Embrace the new winds blowing
Just as flowers bloom and wither in their place
Blooming once again
If you're alive, love with all your might.
