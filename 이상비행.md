## Translation In English
title: 
- Ascent into the Unknown

content:
I am truly simple
Even if I dislike something, I become affectionate
Crying like crazy
But soon wanting to smile
I have had many doubts
About how to live, why I am living
Despite my small stature
Where am I carrying this pain from
Even if this heart
Is said to crash someday
So it doesn't hurt
I would rather let myself go
Even if someone far away
Mocked me
To the background music
I will dance
