## Translation In English
Title:  
D.O. (D.O.) - Ordinary Days of Tomorrow

Content:  
As the end of a long day approaches  
Even though my tired heart has nowhere to go  
Just by leaning on the wind blowing silently  
When I rest my hopes, I receive comfort  
And another day passes  

Memories of happiness suddenly rush in  
In the gaps, I find myself smiling for no reason at night  
Under the moon shining like a street lamp  
If I walk, leaning on my well-drawn friend  
Another day passes  

You and I  
Still crying, laughing, and wandering  
Day and night  
Always waiting as if dreaming  
Before falling asleep, drawing a new day  
Another tomorrow comes  

Today's us  
How will we be remembered in fragments  
Tomorrow's us  
What dreams of youth will we face  
Perhaps in the lonely long night  
If we sketch out the upcoming us  
Another day passes
