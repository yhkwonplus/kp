## Translation In English
Title:  
- Voyage

Content:  
01. On a Voyage

The moon shines bright and merry, thoughts of home naturally arise  
Hoisting the sails with a fair wind, the seagulls squawking overhead  
Seagulls flock, dancing gracefully on the rolling waves  
Fishing boats set out, sounding the horn with gusto  
Venturing forth with bodies swathed in the sea breeze, fighting with seagulls for cover  

Raise the anchor. Hoist the sails.  
Push with vigor, scratch the surface, let's carve a path through the waters  
Let the sound of the waves be drowned out, crash fiercely  
Push and scratch, carve a path through the waters, take a deep breath! Pour out the sea green in the hull  
Then the waves, slapping their heads, wildly leap and try to swallow the ship whole  
Swish over the turbulent blue mountain ranges  

Push! Scratch! Pull!  
Set sail. Rise.  
Wave, you also, rise.  
The sunlight slides down the waves, slicing through them vigorously  
Turbulent seas! What a sight! Crash! Crash!  
The horizon at the edge of the waters is filled  
The dawn spills forth abundantly  

At that moment, the sea of haze dissipates  
Gliding over the water beneath the low-lying mist  
Let your voice ring out loudly, awaken from your slumber and set sail.
