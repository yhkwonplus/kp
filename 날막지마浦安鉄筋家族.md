## Translation In English
Title:
- Don't Block Me (Urayasu Tekkin Kazoku)

Content:
Hey, please just move out of the way
Remove the stone in front of me
On days when nothing goes right, don’t bother me
Leave me alone!
Everyone boasts about how great they are
And ignores and insults someone like me
I'm not a fool who's too scared to speak up
No~
I want to focus only on what’s ahead (ma-ma-ma-ma-ma-ma-mom)
Don't block my way (ma-ma-ma-ma-ma-ma-ma)
Why do I always have bad luck?
Oh! Don't bother me, quickly move aside
Please, just leave me alone~~~
